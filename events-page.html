Anilao Waste-to-Cash Program Kick-Off

March 24-26, 2023

Solitude Acacia Resort, Anilao Batangas

⚠️ SOS: It's time to act now!

Join us as we launch the partnership between Century Tuna and Aling Tindera in Anilao, Batangas! Together with our dear sea saver volunteers, as well as the diving communities, partner agencies and institutions, get ready for an exciting beach bonding to remember, as we spend the day cleaning up and rehabilitating the area's shore, reefs, and general community! All the plastic waste gathered will be properly processed and disposed of via Aling Tindera bins placed on-site at the event area. Plus, with our waste-to-cash program, get the chance to earn some pocket money back for the amount of plastic you collect! Do good by the environment and earn rewards as you go! We'll be welcoming around 50-60 attendees to be part of this special event so don't miss the chance to be part of something big as you create a positive impact on our society. Now's the time to come together and be advocates of preventing marine plastic pollution and becoming champions for cleaner beach destinations!

The move to a circular economy of plastic starts with initiatives like the HOPE’s Aling Tindera program - introducing everyone to the proper approach to plastic waste and learning about the significant benefits that result from reusing or disposing of plastic waste correctly.
